South London Coffee Quest

A self-guided walking tour for coffee lovers in Sydenham and Beckenham.

Explore the Heart of the Local Coffee Scene

Discover four unique and highly-rated coffee shops on a delightful 2.1-mile journey. This walk is designed to introduce you to the best of what the Sydenham and Beckenham areas have to offer, one delicious cup at a time.
